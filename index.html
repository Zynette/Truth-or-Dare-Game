Truth or Dare

Game Instructions

Welcome to the spine-chilling world of Horror Truth or Dare! Prepare yourself for a night filled with suspense, fright, and eerie revelations. Follow these instructions to play the game and embrace the terror:

  1. Enter Player Name: Begin by entering your name in the provided input field. Each player must enter their name before starting their turn. Click the Ready button to proceed.
  2. Flip the Coin: A mysterious coin will appear. Click on the coin to flip it. The coin will reveal your fate: Truth or Dare.
  3. Receive Your Challenge: Depending on the coin's decision, a chilling Truth question or a daunting Dare will appear on the screen. Read your challenge carefully and prepare yourself.
  4. Start the Timer: As soon as the challenge is revealed, the timer will start counting. Complete your challenge as the seconds tick away.
  5. Complete the Challenge: Once you have faced your fear or revealed your darkest secret, click the Done button. The timer will stop, and your performance time will be recorded.
  6. Log Your History: Your name, the challenge, and the time taken will be logged in the history section. The history log will keep a record of all players' challenges and times.
  7. Repeat: Pass the device to the next player and repeat the process from step 1. Continue playing until everyone has faced their fears and revealed their truths.
